## What This Is

This isn't an app. It's a life archive. A visual diary of everywhere I've been, everywhere I've called home, and the people who made it all possible.

Every pin on the map is a memory. Every chapter is a turning point. Every road trip is a story.

## 🔒 Privacy

Everything stays on your device. No servers, no accounts, no tracking. Data stored in browser localStorage.

---

## 📱 Use on iPhone

1. Open the link in **Safari**
2. Tap **Share** (⬆️) → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Opens fullscreen like a native app

---

## 💾 Backup & Sync

- **Journey tab → Export** — downloads JSON backup
- **Journey tab → Import** — load on another device
- Merges without duplicates
